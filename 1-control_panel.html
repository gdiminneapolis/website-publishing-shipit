Ship It

Publishing your website to the world wide web

Class Description

It is time to get your website live on the World Wide Web! This class will give you the tools you need to publish your work, and by the end of the course you will have a url to send to your friends.

Class will cover domains, hosting, and publishing your website. You will need to buy your own domain, and students in this class will receive one year of hosting sponsored by GDI Minneapolis! During class we will help you with your purchases (if you choose to make them) and make sure your GDI hosting is connected to your domain.

By the end of this workshop, you will:

  • Be able to manage your local and remote files and be able to publish (FTP) your files to the world.
  • Be comfortable with purchasing domains and hosting services.
  • Have a website published on the World Wide Web!

Prerequisites:

Intro to HTML/CSS or equivalent course work

Please have the following installed on your Macbook or laptop before class:

• Filezilla • Sublime Text or Atom
